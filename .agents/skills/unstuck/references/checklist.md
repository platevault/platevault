# Unstuck Checklist

- What is the exact failing command?
- What is the smallest reproduction or feedback loop?
- Did `diagnose` run first? If not, why was it skipped?
- What changed most recently?
- Is the error from config, environment, or code?
- What assumption is currently driving the fix?
- What evidence contradicts that assumption?
- What would disprove that assumption quickly?
- What has already been tried, and what happened?
